Booked this tour to learn the history face to face. Tour begins in Independence Hall Visitors Center. Mike walk our group to the City Tavern from there. On our way we met impersonator of the Dunlap and fun begun. During the dinner which served by servers "from 1700's" there was more interactions, we "learned " old Irish song,etc.. After dark we returned into Independence Hall , we had tour of it, watched "debate" about Declaration of Independence. After the tour Mike told us about charity work of the Company. Very impressive. Before we said goodbye everyone received copy of Declaration of Independence. Check them out!!! Lots of fun and help for a good cause, what can be better! !!
